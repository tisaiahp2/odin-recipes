le chocolade moose

Ingredients

  • Moose
  • a large vat of melted chocolate
  • a crane with a net for the moose
  • a crippling lack of sense

Directions

  1. acquire moose
  2. get moose in the fitted net
  3. attach net anchorage to crane
  4. move moose to vat of melted chocolate
  5. submerge moose to taste
  6. clean moose off afterwards
